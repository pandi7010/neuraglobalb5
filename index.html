Tips to Improve Communication Skills

  • Practice active listening and avoid interrupting.
  • Maintain eye contact and use open body language.
  • Think before speaking and be clear and concise.
  • Expand your vocabulary and improve articulation.
  • Engage in conversations regularly to build confidence.
  • Read books, watch speeches, and learn from great communicators.
